Grace White Sherwood

In the 1700's, it was just easier to call her a witch.

She was know as the “Witch of Pungo” - because crops and livestock never, ever, die on their own. Like, ever.

Grace White Sherwood (1660-1740), was the last person known to have been convicted of witchcraft in Virginia.


A farmer, healer, and midwife, she was accused by her neighbors of transforming herself into a cat, damaging crops, and causing the death of livestock.
